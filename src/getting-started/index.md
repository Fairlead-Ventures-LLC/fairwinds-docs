# Getting Started with FairWinds

Welcome to FairWinds! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## Creating an Account

1. Visit [FairWinds](https://fairwinds.app)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address

## Your First Race

Once you've created an account:

1. **Browse Available Races** - Check the race calendar for upcoming events
2. **Join a Race** - Click "Join" on any open race
3. **Set Your Route** - Use the routing tools to plan your course
4. **Start Racing** - When the race begins, your boat will follow your planned route

## Understanding the Interface

### Map View
The main map shows:
- Your boat position
- Other competitors
- Wind vectors
- Current overlays
- Weather systems

### Controls
- **Pan**: Click and drag the map
- **Zoom**: Use mouse wheel or pinch gestures
- **Route Planning**: Click waypoints to set your course
